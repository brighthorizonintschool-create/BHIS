About Bright Horizon International School

Motto : The Sky Is The Limit.

Bright Horizon International School is committed to providing holistic education that nurtures the intellectual, emotional, and social growth of every student. From Crèche to Junior High School, our programs are designed to foster curiosity, creativity, and character.

Our Vision

To provide an inclusive early childhood and basic education that empowers students for lifelong success in academic excellence , character development to succeed in a dynamic world.

Our Mission

To be the beacon of quality education in the community, where every child’s potential shines bright by inspiring,empower students through quality education, innovative teaching methods, and a supportive learning environment that encourages both academic and personal growth.

Core Values

  • Excellence in teaching and learning
  • Respect and integrity
  • Collaboration and teamwork
  • Innovation and creativity
  • Community engagement
